Product Overview

Frosty Knows Best is a smart, energy-saving device powered by microbits, designed to remind users to close their fridge door. It monitors fridge conditions and sends timely alerts to prevent energy wastage, keeping your food fresh and reducing power consumption effortlessly.

This smart device, powered by a series of microbits, monitors the state of your refrigerator in real time, guaranteeing that the door is always closed properly. Frosty Knows Best uses smart sensors to determine whether the refrigerator door is left open or not fully closed. It promptly sends you a sound signal, reminding you to close the door before chilly air escapes. Not only does this save energy, but it also contributes to proper food storage conditions, extending the life of your foods.

Key Features

Real-time Monitoring

Sensors detect temperature, light, and door position to ensure your fridge is closed properly.

Energy Saving Alerts

Audible reminders and visual cues help reduce power consumption by preventing the fridge from staying open.

User-Friendly Display

Easy-to-read notifications displayed on the microbit interface, providing peace of mind.

Compact Design

Fits seamlessly into any fridge without taking up much space.

Benefits

  • Helps save on energy costs by reducing unnecessary power consumption.
  • Prevents food spoilage by maintaining the correct fridge temperature.
  • Convenient, automated system that requires no additional effort from the user.
